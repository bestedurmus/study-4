Hey there, I'm Melanie! I am a product designer that has previously worked on commerce systems, communication tools, and brand identity, and currently lead the commerce design on blabla. I mostly tend to lean into projects that necessitate a high degree of vision, problem solving, and visual polish. In my experience, I've come to find that collaborating with partners & stakeholders intimately throughout the design process creates the best product. I have a knack for problem solving in design, especially on digital platforms. Previously, I've had the opportunity to work as a UX/Visual Design Intern at Blizzard Entertainment, as well as working at Blabla as a UX Design Intern on Gmail. When I'm not designing, you can find me practicing photography,watching movies, creating yet another Spotify playlist.
